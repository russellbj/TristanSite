About Me

My name is Tristan Vaca, I am a 21 year old college student at Beloit College, and I have been studying woodwork since middle school. Since then, I have crafted a number of complex projects, including multiple theater sets, model houses, functional crossbows and longbows, and a number of guitars both electric and acoustic. Many of these can be found on my Projects tab. If you would like to learn more about my work, commission me for a project, or just have a chat, hit me up using the Commission Me tab, or the contact information below. Thanks for taking an interest in my passion. Hopefully I can help you make something beautiful. :)
